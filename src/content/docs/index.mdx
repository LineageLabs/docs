Welcome to the wayID documentation. wayID is trust infrastructure for AI agents, built by [Lineage Labs](https://way.je).

wayID creates a verifiable chain of trust between AI agents and the humans who operate them. By binding verified human identities to registered agents, wayID enables consumers to check who is behind an agent before trusting it.

## How it works

1. **Verify your identity** — Prove you are a real person using zero-knowledge proofs or traditional eID. No personal data is stored.
2. **Register your agents** — Claim your AI agents and bind them to your verified identity using OpenClaw DIDs.
3. **Issue certificates** — Generate a wayID certificate that consumers can check before interacting with your agent.

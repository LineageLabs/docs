Before you can register AI agents, you need to create a verified owner account on WayID. This involves two steps: signing up and verifying your identity.

## Sign up

Create your WayID account using one of the supported OAuth providers:

- **GitHub** — Sign in with your GitHub account
- **Google** — Sign in with your Google account

More login providers are coming soon.

## Identity verification

After signing up, you need to verify your identity. This step proves you are a real person without storing or sharing any personal data.

WayID supports multiple identity verification methods. See the [Identity](/identity/approach/) section for details on how each method works.

Once verified, your account is marked as identity-checked. This verification status is included in certificates issued for your agents.

## Profile information

You can optionally add public profile information to your owner account. This information is visible to consumers who inspect your agents' certificates:

- **Website** — A URL to your personal or company website
- **Social media** — Links to your social media profiles (e.g. X/Twitter, LinkedIn)

This information helps consumers establish additional context about who operates an agent, beyond the cryptographic identity verification.

## What's next

Once your identity is verified, you can [register your AI agents](/certificate/agent-registration/).
